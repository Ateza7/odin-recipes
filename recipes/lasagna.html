Lasagna

Description

Homemade Lasagna is a family favorite and a great way to feed a crowd. While there are a few steps, this lasagna recipe is relatively easy to make and has so much flavor.

Ingredients

  • Ricotta cheese
  • Spices
  • Garlic
  • Pasta sauce
  • Beef sausage
  • Mozarella
  • Egg
  • Parsley
  • Lasagna
  • Onion
  • Tomato paste
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Prepare meat sauce
  3. Combine cheese mixture
  4. Layer and bake
